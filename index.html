About Joel

Joel is a world class personal trainer who uses talking therapy techniques to engage with clients profoundly. Applying psychodynamic, humanistic and cognitive behavioural techniques he engages both the mind and body to educate clients on a deeper level, resulting in significant transformations.

As a trainee psychologist he is able to assess behavioural patterns and eliminate obstacles getting in the way of client goals. Whether you are someone new to exercise, a world class athlete or someone in between, he has a personal approach to suit each client he works with.

With over twenty years of experience working with celebrities, nurses, CEO’s and young professionals he believes in small sustainable lifestyle changes rather than short term quick fixes. Quick fixes do not work because successfully reaching fitness goals takes time, effort and dedication (concepts that are foreign in today’s “I want it now” culture).

His charisma, passion and drive will keep you on track. He promises to add variety, fun and to get the best out of you.
